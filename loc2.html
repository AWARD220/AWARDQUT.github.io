Why the Riverside markets

The Riverside markets are huge collection of stalls and activites run at both QUT Gardens point and the Botinical gardens. There's so much to do here it's almost overwhelming! Come try some great food from all different cultures, get a smoothie on a cold day or a hot chocoalte on a cold one, shop the boutique stores or just soak in the vibes with some free entertainment.

The markets are located on QUT campus and the Botanical gardens every sunday from 6am to 1pm.
