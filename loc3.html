Why the Qut Art Museum

The QUT Art Museum is collection of student created artwork - with constantly changing exhibits of all types! Come check out the current exhibit on creative processeses and get inspired by the talented folks at QUT

The exhibits and the events held in the Art Museum are all completely free! Located in the U-block, take the time to enjoy some good art and refreshing Air conditioning. and don't forget to check out QUT's sister gallery, the William Robinson Gallery, if you have the time.
