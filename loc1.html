Why the cube?

The Cube is a huge digital learning space full of interactive screens and games. Available all year round, The cube shows anything from Dinosaur simulations to digital aquariums. The 48 HD touch screens are awesome to play and interact with for hours of educational fun. Personally, my favourite is the retro arcade, showcasing heaps of cool pixel sports games and the mathematics behind them!

Along with this The Cube also has a number of study spaces to chill and get some work done. Hang out on the second floor to get a better look at the massive screens!
Discover and explore the different parts of The Cube at QUT gardens point Q-block.
